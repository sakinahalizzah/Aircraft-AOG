Definitions
ACTIVE

Flew within the last 3 days.

IDLE T+3

3–6.9 days since last flight. Early warning, not yet AOG proxy.

GROUNDED T+7

7+ days idle, or no flights in the 30-day lookback. The AOG proxy threshold.

STORED

Not auto-detected — requires a manual tag, since flight data alone can't separate storage from AOG.

"Grounded T+7" tracks unexplained fleet inactivity, not verified AOG. Industry usage of "AOG" is specifically unscheduled and mechanical, with no duration threshold — an aircraft is AOG from the fault until return to service, whether that's four hours or four weeks. This proxy cannot distinguish true AOG from scheduled heavy maintenance or storage, since all three look identical as "not flying" in flight-history data. Read the trend and the level against baseline, not this percentage as a literal AOG count. Cross-check quarterly against Capital A Group's disclosed aircraft-in-operation figures to calibrate.
